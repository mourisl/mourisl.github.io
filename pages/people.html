Li Song 11/2022 Principle Investigator Li Song received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University in 2018, and finished the postdoc training in X. Shirley Liu lab and Heng Li lab at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute in 2022. His research area is on computational biology. He has designed and implemented algorithms to analyze next-generation sequencing data, mostly RNA-seq data, for various applications, such as immune repertoire analysis, metagenomics, transcriptome assembly and sequencing read error corrections.
